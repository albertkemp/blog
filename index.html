Luxury aircraft interiors

I like luxury aircraft interiors on many aircraft, such as the 747, a380 and 777 as well as  older planes like the 707, 314 for how airlines used to configure them or still do, in the case of Emirates' a380s.

Boeing 747:

A Pan Am 747-100 arrives from London in 1990 Source: Wikipedia

The Boeing 747 was first introduced more than 50 years ago and stopped being produced only last year. Many airlines have used the 747 for luxury air services, especially on the upper deck, and was the pinnacle of luxury air travel at its time.

 

Many airlines used the upper deck for huge luxury, such as Pan Am's onboard lounge and restaurant:

Source: Emirates

The a380 is really the ultimate luxury air travel experience because of its huge space which allows for airlines to add luxury features like showers, bars and lounges. I am focusing on Emirates' a380s as they seem to offer the most luxury experience.

Source: Emirates

The huge space in the Airbus A380 allows for expansive economy class cabins as well as extremely luxury first and business suites, a bar and lounge and the shower spa.
